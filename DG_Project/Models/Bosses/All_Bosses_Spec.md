# Model Specs — All Bosses
## Isle of Trials — Bosses/

---

## Shared Style for All Bosses
All bosses use: **Low poly stylized 3D, large imposing scale, dramatic silhouette, island-themed corruption, glowing weak points, T-pose or neutral pose, white background, game-ready mesh**

Bosses should look like corrupted ancient guardians — once noble, now twisted by dark energy.

## Boss 0 — Driftwood Golem (Miniboss)
**Island:** Tutorial | **Size:** ~2.5 x 2.0 x 3.0 units | **Target Polys:** 5,000–8,000

### Visual Description
- Massive humanoid figure assembled from shipwrecked timber and driftwood
- Arms are thick rough-hewn planks bolted together
- Head is a ship's figurehead (carved wooden face)
- Chest has a glowing sea-green crystal embedded in the center (the weak point)
- Seaweed and barnacles clinging all over the body
- Held together with old iron bolts, nails, and chains
- Colors: Light tan/grey driftwood, iron grey metal, glowing sea-green crystal

**Island:** Ember Isle | **Size:** ~8.0 x 5.0 x 6.0 units | **Target Polys:** 10,000–20,000

### Visual Description
- Massive serpentine dragon with four clawed legs and large bat-like wings
- Body covered in black volcanic rock scales with bright orange lava cracks between every scale
- The cracks glow intensely, as if the dragon is made of cooled lava barely containing fire inside
- Head: broad dragon skull, swept-back horns, massive jaw with flame-colored interior
- Belly: exposed weak point — glowing bright yellow at the underbelly
- Tail ends in a spiked molten tip
- Wings: membrane stretched between rocky spines, lava veins across the wing surface
- Colors: Matte volcanic black scales, glowing orange/yellow lava cracks, red eyes
