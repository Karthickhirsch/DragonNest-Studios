## 1. Welcome to Isle of Trials

You are Kael, a young sailor who has discovered a mysterious map leading to the lost Archipelago of Aethermoor. A terrible eternal storm has cursed these islands for centuries.

**Your mission:** Sail the seas, discover each island, solve their mysteries, defeat the boss that guards each Tide Crystal, and restore the ancient Aethermoor lighthouse.

The game is an **adventure puzzle game**. Exploration, observation, and problem-solving are just as important as combat. Take your time, look around, and read the environment — it always has clues.

## 3. The Boat

Your boat is your lifeline on the open sea. Keep it in good condition.

### Sailing Basics
- **Steer** with WASD or the left stick.
- **Wind direction** (shown by the weathervane on your compass) affects your speed:
  - Sailing with the wind: **Speed boost**.
  - Sailing against the wind: **Speed reduction**.
  - Use the boost to overcome headwinds temporarily.

### Boat Features

#### Boost
- Tap Shift for a brief speed burst.
- You have **3 boost charges** that recharge over time.
- Use boost to escape sea creatures or ride through hazardous zones quickly.

#### Anchor
- Drop the anchor to stop the boat completely.
- Useful for fishing up floating items or interacting with objects in the water.
- Remember to raise the anchor before sailing again!

#### Harpoon
- Fire the harpoon at sea creatures to deter them.
- Also used to snag floating chests and debris.
- Has a short reload time between shots.

#### Lantern
- Illuminates dark water zones.
- Reveals hidden underwater paths and shallow rocks.
- Some secrets only appear when the lantern is lit.

### Boat Durability
- Shown as the **Ship Wheel icon** in the bottom-left HUD.
- Reduced by collisions with rocks, creatures, and hazards.
- Repairs can be made by:
  - Using a **Repair Kit** from inventory.
  - Docking at an island (auto-repair on island arrival).
  - Finding a Merchant Ship (full repair for coins).
- If durability reaches 0 at sea, you wash up on the nearest island. Rebuild the boat using driftwood (shown on screen).

## 4. Island Exploration

When you spot an island on the horizon, sail toward it. The fog around it will lift as you approach.

### Docking
- A dock or beach will be clearly visible.
- Approach at low speed to avoid hull damage on rocks.
- The game auto-saves when you first land on an island.

### Exploring
- Islands are divided into **areas** connected by paths.
- Some paths are locked behind puzzles — look for clues.
- Press **E** (or Square/X) near objects to interact or inspect.
- Press **J** to open your Journal — important clues are automatically recorded.

### Hidden Secrets
- Look for **cracked walls** (can be broken with a charged attack).
- **Waterfall curtains** often hide cave entrances.
- **Shiny objects on the ground** indicate hidden items.
- Dead ends often have collectibles or lore items.

### NPCs
Some islands have NPC survivors. Talking to them:
- Reveals island backstory.
- Gives hints about puzzle solutions.
- Occasionally starts side quests for bonus rewards.

## 5. Combat

### Basic Combat
- **Attack:** Left click / A button — standard sword swing.
- **Combo:** 3 hits in quick succession = Combo Finish (deals 2x damage).
- **Dodge:** Invincible for a brief moment while dodging. Time it against incoming attacks.
- **Knockback:** Some attacks knock enemies back — useful near ledges.

### Tips for Combat
- **Watch enemy animations** — every attack has a wind-up. If you see a big swing, dodge.
- **Dodge toward enemies** (not away) for melee enemies — it puts you behind them.
- **Ranged enemies** should be closed in on quickly — they're weak up close.
- **Grouped enemies:** AoE charged attack hits all of them at once.

### Charged Attack
- Hold the attack button for 1.5 seconds → release for a powerful charged strike.
- Breaks enemy guards and deals 3x damage.
- Drains a small amount of stamina.

### Status Effects
| Effect | Description | Cure |
|--------|-------------|------|
| **On Fire** | Lose HP over time | Roll in water; use Water Flask |
| **Frozen** | Movement slowed; dodge range reduced | Fire Dash (or wait 5s) |
| **Poisoned** | HP drains slowly | Antidote Berry or Antidote item |
| **Stunned** | Cannot act briefly | Wait it out |
| **Electrified** | Take extra damage from next hit | Grounding Rod item |

## 6. Puzzles

Puzzles are the heart of Isle of Trials. Here's how to approach them:

### General Approach
1. **Look around the full room** before touching anything.
2. **Press E/Inspect** on all objects — even background ones may have clues.
3. **Check your Journal (J)** — clues found are automatically recorded.
4. **Try things** — most puzzles can be reset safely.

### Common Puzzle Types

#### Symbol Matching
- Rotate tiles to match a pattern shown somewhere in the room.
- The solution is always visible in the environment — look on walls, ceilings, or floors.

#### Light Beams
- Rotate mirrors to direct beams of light to receivers.
- Follow the beam to see where it goes wrong, then work backwards.

#### Sequence Puzzles
- Objects must be activated in a specific order.
- Order is hinted at by numbers, paintings, star patterns, or sounds.

#### Push Puzzles
- Shove blocks onto pressure plates.
- Think ahead — blocks can only be pushed (not pulled).
- If stuck, move away from the room and re-enter to reset block positions.

### Hint System
Stuck on a puzzle? Here's how to get help:
1. **Inspect nearby objects** — there's always at least one hint nearby.
2. **Wait 3 minutes** — the game gives a gentle nudge.
3. **Use a Hint Token** (from inventory) for a direct hint.
4. **Enable Help Mode** in Settings for full step-by-step guidance.

## 7. The Map

Press **M** to open your parchment map.

### Map Features
- **Discovered areas** are shown clearly; undiscovered areas have fog.
- **Your position** is shown as a boat icon (at sea) or a compass rose (on island).
- **Island icons** appear when discovered:
  - Gray = Not completed
  - Half-color = In progress
  - Full color = Boss defeated / Crystal collected
- **Question marks** indicate rumors of undiscovered islands (from bottles/NPCs).
- **Danger zones** on the sea are shown with skull icons.

### Navigation Tips
- Use the map to plan your route between islands.
- Avoid skull zones until you have boat upgrades.
- The Merchant Ship location updates on the map when nearby.

## 9. Health & Survival

### Health
- Shown as a **life ring** in the top-left corner.
- Maximum: 100 HP (upgradeable to 200 HP).
- Regenerates slowly when out of combat (3 HP/sec after 5s of no damage).
- Heal with potions or sitting at campfires on islands.

### Dying
- If HP reaches 0, Kael collapses.
- You respawn at the last checkpoint on that island (or at your boat if at sea).
- No items are lost on death.
- Enemies partially recover HP when you respawn.

### Checkpoints
- Campfires on islands serve as checkpoints — rest at them to set a respawn point.
- Puzzle rooms save progress automatically (solved puzzles stay solved after death).
- Boss fights restart from full HP (both yours and the boss).

## 10. Abilities

You earn one **special ability** by defeating each island's boss. These abilities are permanent and used both in combat and for traversal.

| Ability | Unlocked After | Description | Use in Exploration |
|---------|---------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Fire Dash** | Defeating Ignar | Dash forward in flames; damages enemies | Melt ice obstacles, ignite torches |
| **Ice Shield** | Defeating Kryss | Block with ice barrier; reflect projectiles | Freeze water to cross; cool lava |
| **Vine Grapple** | Defeating Virenax | Grapple to anchor points | Swing across gaps, reach high areas |
| **Sand Veil** | Defeating Phareth | Turn briefly invisible | Slip past enemies; hidden path activator |
| **Lightning Strike** | Defeating Zephyrath | Slam lightning down on target area | Power ancient machines; stun enemies |

### Ability Cooldowns
- All abilities have a **cooldown** shown as a circular timer on your HUD ability icon.
- Cooldowns range from 5–15 seconds.
- Cooldown is reduced by upgrades found in chests.

## 11. Boss Fights

Each island's boss guards a Tide Crystal. Defeating it is required to progress.

### General Boss Fight Rules
1. **Study the arena before the fight** — it always has clues about the boss's weakness.
2. **Phase transitions** — when the boss hits certain HP thresholds, it changes behavior. Stay alert.
3. **Weakness items** — usually found in the arena. Save them for the right moment.
4. **Staying mobile** — standing still is dangerous. Always be ready to dodge.
5. **Boss health bar** — appears at the top of the screen during the fight.

### If You're Struggling
- Lower the difficulty in Settings (no penalty or achievement loss).
- Make sure you're using items in your inventory.
- The weakness of each boss is hinted at in the arena and in your Journal.
- Watch attack animations — every attack has a clear telegraph before it activates.

## 12. Sea Creatures

The ocean is not empty. Various creatures patrol the waters between islands.

### Encountering Creatures

| Creature | Behavior | How to Handle |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| Coral Jellyfish | Drifts; stuns on contact | Steer around them |
| Razorfin Shark | Circles, then rams | Outrun with boost; harpoon to deter |
| Tide Serpent | Lunges, can coil boat | Harpoon head during surface attack |
| Frost Crab | Latches onto boat | Fire harpoon at claw to detach |
| Abyssal Kraken | Grabs boat tentacles | Harpoon all tentacles repeatedly |

### Sea Creature Zones
- **Easy waters** (near Tutorial Island): No creatures.
- **Medium waters**: Sharks and jellyfish.
- **Hard waters** (storm belt, abyss): Krakens, Leviathan.

## 13. Tips & Tricks

### Exploration
- **Always check behind waterfalls** — they hide cave entrances.
- **Messages in bottles** found at sea contain puzzle hints for nearby islands.
- **Talk to every NPC** — their stories unlock lore and sometimes bonus quests.
- **Light your lantern** at sea at night — it reveals hidden shallow paths to secret coves.

### Combat
- **Parrying:** If you attack exactly when an enemy attacks, it triggers a parry (2x damage window for 2 seconds).
- **Environmental kills:** Many arenas have edges, lava pools, or pits. Knock enemies in.
- **Combo chaining:** After a dodge roll, the attack immediately after has +20% damage (Dash Attack).

### Puzzles
- **The answer is always in the room.** If you haven't found the clue, keep exploring.
- **Reset is free** — never be afraid to reset a puzzle if you're confused.
- **Nighttime puzzles**: Some puzzles require daytime or nighttime — camp at a fire to wait.

### Resource Management
- **Keep at least 1 Repair Kit** at all times before entering unknown waters.
- **Save Health Potions** for boss fights — regular enemies can be avoided.
- **Coins add up** — sell rare drops to merchants; upgrades matter in later islands.

## 14. Accessibility Options

Isle of Trials includes several accessibility settings:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Help Mode** | Auto-provides step-by-step puzzle hints after timeout |
| **Extended Timing Windows** | Doubles timing window on dodge and timing puzzles |
| **High Contrast Mode** | Brightens all puzzle clues, highlights interactable objects |
| **Auto-Aim Assist** | Harpoon and ranged attacks auto-aim to nearest target |
| **Reduced Camera Shake** | Minimizes screen shake effects |
| **Text Size** | Small / Medium / Large |
| **Subtitle Size** | Small / Medium / Large |
| **Colorblind Mode** | Adjusts color palette for Deuteranopia / Protanopia / Tritanopia |
| **Difficulty Presets** | Easy / Normal / Hard / Challenge |
| **Invincibility Mode** | Player cannot take damage (for story-focused playthrough) |

All settings can be changed at any time from the Pause Menu → Settings without penalty.
